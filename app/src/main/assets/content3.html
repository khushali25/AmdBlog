If you know me at all, then you probably know that I’m not a vegan. While I do respect the lifestyle, and try to make vegan decisions as much as possible, I’d be lying if I said I’m totally, 110% vegan.

But like I said, I do try to make vegan food choices as much as possible… and I do get super excited whenever something cool, new, and vegan comes out in Montreal.Just like Hoche Glace (2225 Avenue Bennett), a new ice cream spot in Montreal’s Hochelaga-Maisonneuve.

This new shop is already making waves for their delicious and super high-quality ice cream… but that’s not the only appeal of this spot, friends.
They also happen to serve some of the tastiest vegan ice cream in the whole city, guys.

Not only is the soft-serve ice cream itself vegan, but you can also dip it into vegan chocolate, and then grab some vegan sorbet to top it off.

 

Or, if vegan soft-serve dipped into vegan chocolate and topped off with some vegan sorbet isn’t your jam, worry not.

They’ve also got vegan hard ice cream, vegan tourbillons, and much more. According to a comment on a Facebook post on their page, they can pretty much make any ice cream treat into a vegan version.
